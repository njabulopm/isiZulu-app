Izikhathi - Tenses


  • Isikhathi esidlulile - Past tense - The verb will end with ile and ele
  • Isikhathi samanje - Present tense - There is no change to verb ending
  • Isikhathi esizayo - Future tense - The verb will have a zo as a prefix
Isenzo-verb Isikhathi esidlulile Isikhathi samanje Isikhathi esizayo
Idla - eat dlile - ate idla zodla - will/shall eat
Hamba - go/leave hambile - went/left hamba - go/leave zohamba - will/shall go/leave
Khuluma - talk/speak khulumile - talked/spoke khuluma - talk/speak zokhuluma - will/shall talk/speak
Bhukuda - swim bhukudile - swam bhukuda - swim zobhukuda - will/shall swim
