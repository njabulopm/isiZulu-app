Bb

ba be bi bo bu
bha bhe bhi bho bhu

Izibonelo-examples

  • Ubaba - father
  • baba - as in Hot chilli
  • buka - look
  • bheka - look/watch

Cc

ca ce ci co cu
cha che chi cho chu
cwa cwe cwi cwo cwu
chwa chwe chwi chwo chwu

Izibonelo-examples

  • caca/cacile - clear
  • cacisa - clarify
  • chaza - explain
  • chazela - explain to another person
  • Ucwaningo - Research/survey
  • cwaninga - the act of researching/surveying
  • Ukucwasa - to discriminate
  • Ichweba - port
